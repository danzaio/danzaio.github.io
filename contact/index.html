good brief format

Goal: what should the LLM or agent workflow accomplish?

Context: which sources, examples, and constraints ground it?

Tools: what can run, what needs approval, and what is off limits?

Proof: what evaluation, reviewer check, surface review, or stop rule would prove it?

Handoff: table, XLSX, code, plan, proof packet, or closeout?

What to send.

Concrete inputs beat polished pitch language. The first pass is about the prompt contract, context sources, examples, tool constraints, approval rules, validation target, artifact format, and safe boundary.

Do not send secrets.

Dan can scope from public safe source links, summaries, safe samples, and explicit approval rules. Keep credentials and sensitive raw data out of email.
